# Valura UAE — Product Context

## Why This Exists
UAE banks face a challenge: customers are financially under-educated, leading to poor product decisions, defaults, and churn. Valura UAE is a B2B white-label module banks can embed or deploy alongside their app to drive engagement, loyalty, and financial literacy — without running afoul of CBUAE advisory rules.

## Problems It Solves
1. **Financial literacy gap** — UAE residents (locals + expats) often lack foundational money knowledge
2. **Bank disengagement** — Most banking apps are transactional; no reason to return daily
3. **Expat complexity** — Remittance, EOSB/gratuity, multi-currency needs are unique and poorly served
4. **Islamic finance confusion** — Dual-track content removes the barrier for observant customers

## How It Works
1. User lands on a cinematic opening (Rive/GSAP animated)
2. Selects avatar protagonist (journeying figure / home / garden)
3. Enters the **Level Map** — an animated SVG road with 15 world nodes
4. Each world = 5–8 quiz nodes + explainer cards
5. Earns VAL COINS, badges, unlocks cosmetics
6. Social layer: friend boards (private), family group quests, pair streaks, weekly tournaments
7. Mastery reviews at 7/30/90 days reinforce retention

## User Experience Goals
- Feels like a game, not a chore
- Fast — nodes complete in < 3 minutes
- Culturally inclusive — 4 cast characters representing the UAE's demographic mix
- Dual language — seamless EN ↔ AR switch with true RTL layout
- Regulatory trust — clear "Educational only" framing throughout
- Zero gambling mechanics — no spin wheels, no loot boxes, no prize draws

## Antagonists
The antagonists are **habits**, not people. Financial behaviours like impulse spending, ignoring EOSB, not understanding credit scores are the "enemies" in the narrative.
